Welcome to Ryan's Website!

Hello! My name is Ryan Yost, I am 24 years old and I attend Sault College. In my free time I enjoy playing video games on my computer and going to the gym. I play a variety of video games but my favourite kind of games are competetive shooting games. I like this style of game because they can get very intense and spike adrenaline in certain situations. I play with a small group of friends, none of which I have ever met in person but I would consider them some of my closest friends.

Alongside my video game habits, I also try to attend the gym on a regular basis. I try to stay in the best possible shape because I play semi-pro football. The league I play in is called the NFC and I play the quarterback position for the Sault Steelers. The NFC is a league where teams all across Northern Ontario go head to head in a six game season. Last year we made it to the semi-finals where we eneded up losing to the Tri-City Outlaws.
